CHRISTMAS SPIRIT BOX



This 'Christmas Spirit Box' was developed by the folks at Liminal Earth a map of the strange, wonderful, unusual, and unexpected. This stream takes cut up and unrecognizable 500ms audio samples from beloved Christmas music and plays them in random order. Use this in place of a traditional spirit box should you need to hold a seance with Santa (or Mrs.) Claus, "the elves", "the reindeer" or any of the various 'ghosts' of Christmas Past/Present/Future.
